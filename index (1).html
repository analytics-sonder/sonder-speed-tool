Why website speed matters for SEO in 2025

Google uses Core Web Vitals as a confirmed ranking factor. Sites that load faster rank higher, retain more visitors, and convert at a higher rate. Our free website speed test gives you the same data as Google PageSpeed Insights вҖ” powered by AI analysis.

What does this website speed test measure?

This tool analyses the key performance metrics that Google's Lighthouse and Core Web Vitals framework use to evaluate your pages:

LCP (Largest Contentful Paint) вҖ” How fast your main content loads. Target: under 2.5 seconds.

FCP (First Contentful Paint) вҖ” When the first content appears on screen. Target: under 1.8 seconds.

TBT (Total Blocking Time) вҖ” How long your page is unresponsive to user input. Target: under 200 ms.

CLS (Cumulative Layout Shift) вҖ” How much your page layout jumps around. Target: under 0.1.

TTI (Time to Interactive) вҖ” When your page becomes fully interactive. Target: under 3.8 seconds.
